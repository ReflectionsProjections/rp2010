Academic Stimulus Package
Presenter
  • Jorge Cham
Description
In this talk, Jorge Cham recounts his experiences traveling to over 150 universities and research centers in the US and across the world, lecturing and listening to graduate students from all walks of life share their stories of academic anxiety and de-motivation. Thought-provoking, yet humorous, Jorge Cham's talk examines the possible effects of the current economic environment on global research and helps students figure out what their role in the coming social order might be.
