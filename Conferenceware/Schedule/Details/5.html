Name
I Want my LOLCat Videos Faster
Presenter
  • Greg Schechter
Description
A talk about Front-end Performance at YouTube.

With two billion views a day, a lot of time is wasted while a video loads. The faster we can get the video to you, the more opportunities you’ll have to watch that tenth LOLCat video. About 80% of the “Why is this taking so long?” page load time is based on front-end choices. This talk is all about how to make web pages faster. Greg Schechter will cover the fast and easy, including gzipping your resources, to the heavy lifting of building our video player in HTML5.
