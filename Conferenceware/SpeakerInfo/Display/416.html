Biography
Dan Peterson is a Product Manager at Google with a focus on developer platforms and a passion for open source projects. Most recently, Dan worked on Google Wave to grow the developer community and promote the federation protocol. Prior to that, Dan helped establish the OpenSocial specification, Apache Shindig, and co-founded the OpenSocial Foundation. Previously, Dan guided the Google Web Toolkit (GWT) team as it became an open source project

and worked on Google's infrastructure team on web search and data center management. Dan earned a B.S. in Computer Science from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, as well as minors in Technology & Management and philosophy.
