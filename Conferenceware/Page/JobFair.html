UIUC Job Fair


The Reflections | Projections Job Fair will be held from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM on Friday, October 15, 2010 at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign campus in the Thomas M. Siebel Center for Computer Science.

The job fair features several premier computing and technology employers offering internships and full-time employment for UIUC students. Please contact corporate@acm.uiuc.edu for more information.
